About

Pull Up Boiiz, a platform founded by Jordan (Jp) and James Jacobs (Juic3) in November 2021, is dedicated to supporting emerging artists in the music industry. With a shared love for music, Jp and Juic3 established Pull Up Boiiz to provide affordable access to performance opportunities and exposure for up-and-coming musicians. The platform serves as a central hub for showcasing emerging talent and empowering artists to succeed through their creativity and hard work. Join us at Pull Up Boiiz as we champion the next generation of music stars and shape the future of the industry
